The origins of Auto-Kenya

Auto-Kenya was founded in 1993 by Omwenga Ian. It was a typical small family business. Initially, it dealt with cars, but in 1993 the company started trading car parts mainly for Japanese and Korean cars.

Business development

When the development opportunities began to increase in 1997, the younger brother, Dariusz Czyżak, joined the company. The company still exists in this shape, ie a civil law partnership. Following the expanding product range, in 2000 the company moved its headquarters to a new location.

2000 m2 area warehouse

Specialization

The company specializes in selling used parts for Japanese cars, mainly Toyota, Nissan, Honda, Suzuki, Mazda, Mitsubishi, Subaru, and also in trading parts for Korean Kia and Hyundai cars. Auto-Japan has become one of the industry leaders in the region.

Over 15,000 parts in stock

Wide offer

The permanent offer includes body parts, headlights, windows, mirrors, doors, fenders, plastic parts, dummy parts, mechanical parts such as engines, gearboxes, alternators, starters, radiators, ignition devices and suspension components: shock absorbers, springs, rocker arms, etc.

Close to the customer

We sell parts for many car brands, at the company's headquarters and by mail order. Part of our assortment can be found on the OLX portal.
